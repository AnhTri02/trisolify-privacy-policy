Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Trisolify provides professional Shopify solutions designed to optimize the e-commerce experience for both B2B and B2C merchants. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data across our suite of applications, including Momsify and Popsify.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

At Trisolify, we build tools that empower entrepreneurs without compromising privacy. Our apps are engineered to perform their functions without collecting, storing, or sharing personal identifiable information (PII) from your customers.

2. App-Specific Data Access

Momsify

Accesses product and store configuration data to perform dynamic price conversions (VAT inclusion/exclusion). This ensures compliance with international tax regulations and Google Merchant Center requirements without accessing customer names or emails.

Popsify

Accesses real-time cart totals to calculate the remaining balance required to reach free delivery goals. This data is used solely for session-based calculations.
